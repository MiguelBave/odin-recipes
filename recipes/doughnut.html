Doughnut

Description

Frying your own doughnuts is definitely a Power Grandma move. Using a can of biscuit dough makes it a project simple enough for anyone to tackle.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts oil for deep frying
  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated buttermilk biscuit dough
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Separate out the biscuits and cut a hole in the middle of each one, like you would a doughnut.
  3. Carefully slide doughnuts into hot oil. Fry until golden brown on both sides. Drain briefly on wire racks placed over baking sheets.
  4. Place the confectioners' sugar in a bowl and roll the hot doughnuts in the sugar.
